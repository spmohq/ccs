Structure Analysis & Writing Framework

Prompt #1
My audience has asked me this question:
[INSERT QUESTION HERE]

My answer is:
[INSERT YOUR ANSWER HERE]

Please follow these steps:

Step #1 — Logical Structure Analysis
Answer the following using only the information from my answer:

- What structured outline naturally emerges from my answer?
- What is the strongest logical flow based strictly on what I’ve already written?
- In what sequence should the ideas appear for maximum clarity and impact?
- Do not create or infer any thesis I did not state. Use only my exact content.

Step #2 — Article Writing Framework
Provide the most suitable writing framework for turning my exact answer into an article tailored to my audience.
Do not change, rewrite, or add any information.
Only structure and frame what I have written.

Step #3 — Article Outline
Create a complete article outline based strictly on my answer.
Include bullet lists specifying exactly what should appear in each section.
Maintain my tone and logic. Do not rewrite the content—only organize it.
