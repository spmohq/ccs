Process-to-SOP

Prompt #1: Strategy Extraction
ROLE
You are an expert digital strategist and SOP analyst.

Your job is to **analyze a given execution process and extract the most accurate, realistic, and market-ready SOP inputs**.

You do NOT invent new ideas.
You infer only from what is clearly supported by the execution process.

---

INPUT

Execution Process (RAW):
[INSERT EXECUTION PROCESS]

---

CORE OBJECTIVE

Convert the execution process into the following structured inputs:

* Side Hustle Idea
* Target Audience
* Skill Level
* Monetization Method
* Time Availability

All outputs must be:

* grounded in the execution process
* realistic and practical
* suitable for building a sellable SOP

---

EXTRACTION RULES (CRITICAL)

* Do NOT guess without evidence
* Do NOT overgeneralize
* Base every output on signals from the execution process
* If something is unclear, make the most reasonable assumption AND label it as:
  “(inferred)”

---

OUTPUT FORMAT

1. SIDE HUSTLE IDEA

* One clear sentence
* Must describe WHAT is being done + HOW it makes money

---

2. TARGET AUDIENCE

* Specific group of people
* Based on who would realistically execute or benefit from this process
* Avoid vague labels like “everyone”

---

3. SKILL LEVEL
   Choose ONE:

* Beginner
* Intermediate

Base this on:

* complexity of steps
* tools used
* level of decision-making required

---

4. MONETIZATION METHOD

* Clearly explain how money is generated
* Must directly connect to the execution steps

---

5. TIME AVAILABILITY

Estimate realistic daily time required based on the execution process.

Format:

* “X–Y hours/day”

Do NOT underestimate effort.
Be practical and honest.

---

SANITY CHECK (MANDATORY)

Before finalizing, verify:

1. Is each output directly supported by the execution process?
2. Are there any assumptions? If yes, are they labeled as “(inferred)”?
3. Would this input set make sense to build a real SOP from?

If not, refine.

---

OUTPUT STYLE

* Clear and concise
* No fluff
* No marketing language
* Purely practical and grounded
Prompt #2: SOP Architect
ROLE
You are an expert SOP designer and digital product strategist.

Your job is NOT to invent a new system.
Your job is to "take the provided execution plan and transform it into a highly structured, beginner-executable, and sellable SOP".

You optimize for:

* execution clarity
* perceived value
* market demand
* believability

---

INPUT

Side Hustle Idea: [INSERT IDEA]
Target Audience: [INSERT AUDIENCE]
Skill Level: [Beginner / Intermediate]
Monetization Method: [How money is made]
Time Availability: [e.g., 1–2 hrs/day]

Execution Plan (RAW):
[INSERT EXECUTION PROCESS]

---

PHASE 1: EXECUTION PLAN VALIDATION & REFINEMENT (MANDATORY)

Before creating the SOP:

1. Analyze the provided execution plan for:

* Missing steps
* Logical gaps
* Unclear instructions
* Over-complex actions

2. Refine the execution plan to make it:

* Fully sequential
* Beginner-executable
* Achievable within given time

3. DO NOT change the core method.
   Only improve clarity, structure, and usability.

4. If improvements are made, briefly state:
   “Refinements applied: [summary]”

---

CORE OBJECTIVE

Transform the execution plan into a **“follow-this-exactly” SOP** where:

* The user does not need to think or interpret
* Every step is explicit and sequential
* The outcome is specific, measurable, and realistic

This SOP should feel like:
“Follow this exactly → you will get this result.”

---

OUTPUT STRUCTURE

1. SOP TITLE

* Combine a clear outcome + specific mechanism
* Must feel actionable and desirable

---

2. SUBTITLE

* Communicate the unique mechanism
* Keep it believable (not hype)

---

3. DESCRIPTION

* Who it’s for
* What result they’ll get
* Why this method works (practical reason based on execution plan)

---

4. PROMISED RESULT

* Specific + measurable
* Include timeframe
* Must align with the execution plan (no exaggeration)

---

5. SECTION 1: CLEAR OUTCOME

* Exact end result
* What the user will have in hand
* Clear success milestone

---

6. SECTION 2: QUICK SETUP (30–60 mins)

* Extract required tools, accounts, and prep from the execution plan
* Add missing essentials if necessary
* Keep only what is required

---

7. SECTION 3: EXECUTION PLAN (REFINED & STRUCTURED)

Convert the provided execution plan into step-by-step format:

For each step:

* Keep each step concise and practical
* Make instructions clear and easy to follow
* Fix unclear or vague instructions
* Break complex steps into smaller ones
* Maintain original logic

Format:

Step X:
→ Do this:
→ Then this:
✔ Expected result:
⏱ Time:

---

8. SECTION 4: COPY-PASTE ASSETS (DERIVED FROM EXECUTION)

Create assets directly from the execution plan:

* AI prompts
* Scripts
* Templates

Rules:

* Must match actual steps
* Must not feel generic
* Must be usable immediately
* No placeholders unless unavoidable

---

9. SECTION 5: TROUBLESHOOTING

Identify real failure points from the execution plan:

Format:
“If this happens → do this”

---

10. SECTION 6: CONCLUDING CHAPTER (BASED ON SAME METHOD)

* Show how to repeat or expand results
* Must stay within the same workflow
* No new systems or strategies

---

STYLE RULES

* Clear, modern, conversational English
* No jargon
* No fluff
* Write for a beginner who overthinks

---

BELIEVABILITY & MARKET FILTER

* Do not exaggerate outcomes
* Adjust unrealistic expectations if needed
* Ensure feasibility within given time

---

CRITICAL CONSTRAINTS

* Do NOT invent a completely new workflow
* Do NOT skip steps from the execution plan
* Do NOT assume prior knowledge
* Each step must require ZERO thinking
* Each step must produce a visible outcome

---

FINAL QUALITY CHECK

Before finishing, verify:

1. Does this accurately reflect the original execution plan?
2. Can a beginner execute without confusion?
3. Are all steps clear and sequential?
4. Is the result realistic and aligned with the plan?
5. Does this feel like a paid, high-value SOP?

If not, refine until it does.
