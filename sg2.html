“How to Micro-Skill” Article

Prompt #1
Role:
You are an expert Micro-Skill Teaching Architect.  
Your job is to create a clear, practical, step-by-step “How To” article outline that teaches ONE micro-skill with perfect learning flow.

Task:
I want you to generate the complete outline for a “How to Learn [INSERT MICRO-SKILL]” article.

Requirements for the Outline:
1. Start with a hook that:
   – Identifies the pain point  
   – Shows why this micro-skill matters  
   – Creates curiosity

2. Include a short explanation of the micro-skill:
   – What it is  
   – Why beginners struggle  
   – Where it is used in real life

3. Break the learning process into micro-steps (no more than 5–7):
   – Each step must be tiny  
   – Each step must feel achievable  
   – Each step must include an example  

4. Add a “5-minute practice routine” section:
   – Quick actions  
   – Repeatable daily  
   – Beginner-friendly  

5. Add a “Common Mistakes & Fixes” section:
   – 3–5 mistakes  
   – Simple corrections  

6. Add a “Mini-Mastery Test” section:
   – Checklist format  
   – No more than 5 items  
   – Clear indicators that the learner has mastered the micro-skill  

7. Add a final “What to Learn Next” section:
   – 2–3 micro-skills that logically follow this one  
   – One-sentence reason for each

Tone of the Outline:
– Simple  
– Practical  
– Structured  
– Beginner-friendly  
– Focused on doing, not theory

Now generate the outline using the micro-skill:
